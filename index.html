Welcome to R-NOT

A clinical research study exploring new approaches to supporting people with PTSD.

The study aims to understand whether nitrous oxide may help reduce the impact of distressing memories.

  • The R-NOT Study is funded by the Medical Research Council (MRC) [Grant Ref: MR/Z503654/1].
  • The study is sponsored by University College London (Sponsor Number: 178733) and delivered through PRIMENT Clinical Trials Unit.
  • The views expressed are those of the research team and not necessarily those of the Medical Research Council.
