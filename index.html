Welcome to the R-NOT for PTSD website!

Retrieval-dependent Nitrous Oxide Therapy.

Retrieval-dependent Nitrous Oxide Therapy (R-NOT) is a new treatment approach being studied for the first time.

This study aims to find out whether R-NOT helps to reduce symptoms of Posttraumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD).

  • The R-NOT Study is funded by the Medical Research Council (MRC) [Grant Ref: MR/Z503654/1].
  • The study is sponsored by University College London (Sponsor Number: 178733) and delivered through PRIMENT Clinical Trials Unit.
  • Trial Registration Number: ISRCTN12553238.
