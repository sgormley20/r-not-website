Wider Team

Anne Marie Downey

Head of Clinical Trials Operations at PRIMENT

Anne Marie Downey is the Head of Clinical Trials Operations at PRIMENT Clinical Trials Unit, where she leads the strategic and operational delivery of complex clinical trial projects in primary care and mental health. Anne Marie has over 24 years experience in clinical trial operations with extensive knowledge in the clinical trial regulations and other research governance frameworks. She leads multidisciplinary teams, foster collaboration, and successfully drives the operational delivery of several high-quality clinical trials, including the R-NOT trial.

Irwin Nazareth

Professor at PRIMENT

Irwin Nazareth is Professor of Primary Care & Population Sciences and Director of PRIMENT CTU at UCL. He has special expertise in design and evaluation of complex intervention in mental health. As a trialist on R-NOT offers support on the design, conduct, methodology, and evaluation of the trial.

Georges Iskander

Consultant Anaesthetist

Consultant Anaesthetist and Lead for Visiting Doctors at UCLH; Honorary Lecturer in Clinical Education and Health Psychology at UCL. Chief Investigator of the R-NOT trial, with clinical and research interests in Endocrine Anaesthesia, Perioperative Medicine, and Psychopharmacology. On a personal level, I serve as a deacon in the Church of England and speak English, Arabic, Italian, and French.
