The R-NOT study is delivered by a multidisciplinary team of researchers, clinicians, and trial specialists with expertise in mental health, clinical trials, and patient care.

Together, the team is committed to conducting high-quality research that is safe, ethical, and participant-focused.

Please use the sidebar to learn more about the people involved in delivering the study.
