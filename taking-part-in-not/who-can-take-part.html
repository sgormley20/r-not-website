Are you Eligible?

Overview

The R-NOT study is looking for adults with PTSD who are awaiting treatment through NHS Talking Therapies and are not currently receiving therapy.

Participation is completely voluntary, and deciding not to take part will not affect your NHS care.

Eligibility criteria

  • Aged 18 to 65
  • Waiting for PTSD treatment through NHS Talking Therapies
  • Not currently receiving psychological therapy
  • Have not changed, and do not plan to change, any mental health medication in the last 4 weeks

Recruitment process

You will be contacted through your NHS Talking Therapies service if your provider thinks you may be eligible.

Your provider will give you further information about the study.

If you decide to take part, the research team will contact you.

Voluntary participation

Taking part is entirely your choice.

If you agree, you will be asked to sign an electronic consent form.

Choosing not to take part, or withdrawing at any time, will not affect the care you receive from NHS Talking Therapies.
