Who Can Take Part?

The R-NOT study is looking for adults with PTSD who are awaiting treatment through NHS Talking Therapies Services (NHS-TTSs).

Participation is completely voluntary, and deciding not to take part will not affect your NHS care.

We are committed to making this study inclusive and accessible, and we welcome people from all backgrounds, including those who are often under-represented in research.

Eligibility criteria

  • Aged 18 to 65.
  • Waiting for PTSD treatment through NHS-TTSs.
  • Are medically suitable for treatment with nitrous oxide.

Recruitment process

If an individual is interested in the study and is eligible, consent can be taken by a member of the team at the collaborating NHS-TTSs.

Once consent has been given, the individual will be referred to the R-NOT study team at UCL for the rest of their participation and have their eligibility confirmed.

Voluntary participation

Participation in this study is entirely voluntary.

Individuals who agree to take part will be asked to complete an electronic consent form.

Choosing not to participate, or withdrawing from the study at any time, will not affect the care received from NHS-TTSs.

  • The R-NOT Study is funded by the Medical Research Council (MRC) [Grant Ref: MR/Z503654/1].
  • The study is sponsored by University College London (Sponsor Number: 178733) and delivered through PRIMENT Clinical Trials Unit.
  • Trial Registration Number: ISRCTN12553238.
