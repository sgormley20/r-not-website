Patient and Public Involvement

Overview

The R-NOT study has been shaped with input from people with lived experience of PTSD.

Patient and Public Involvement (PPI) has been part of every stage, helping us make the study safe, understandable, and relevant to participants.

Our Patient Advisory Group

The study has a dedicated Patient Advisory Group made up of people with experience of PTSD.

  • They provide insight into what it is like to live with intrusive memories and other PTSD symptoms
  • They help ensure that study materials, procedures, and communications are clear and sensitive

How PPI has shaped the study

  • Advising on the study design and treatment procedures
  • Reviewing participant information sheets, consent forms, and this website
  • Providing guidance on how to make the treatment visits and home monitoring as comfortable as possible

Why their input matters

  • Gives the research team a real-world perspective on living with PTSD
  • Helps make the study more participant-friendly, safe, and effective
  • Ensures the study addresses issues that matter most to people with PTSD

The R-NOT Study is funded by the Medical Research Council (MRC) [Grant Ref: MR/Z503654/1].
