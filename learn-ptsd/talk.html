Need Someone to Talk to?

If you are feeling overwhelmed, distressed, or need someone to talk to, there are trusted organisations you can contact for support.

All of the services listed below are free and confidential.

If you need urgent help, please contact your GP or call 111.

If you need to call someone for support, you can call:

Mind’s support line (9am-6pm) at 0300 102 1234

Samaritans (24/7, 365 days a year) on 116 123

SANEline (4:30pm-10pm everyday) on 0300 304 7000

National Suicide Prevention Helpline UK (6pm to midnight every day) on 0800 689 5652

Campaign Against Living Miserably (CALM) (5pm–midnight every day) on 0800 58 58 58
